Use this service to apply as an individual under section 1088 to remove your home address, or part of it, from the public Companies House register.

You can use this service if you are (or were) a:

  • company officer
  • person with significant control (PSC)
  • subscriber or member of a company, LLP, societas Europaea (SE) or eligible Scottish partnership
  • officer or permanent representative of an overseas company

You'll need:

  • to know which documents contain your home address.Check this by finding your company on the Companies House register.
  • the completed SR01 form
  • to save the completed form to the device you are using
