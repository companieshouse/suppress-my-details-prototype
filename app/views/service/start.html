Use this service to apply to remove a home address, or part of it, from a document on the Companies House public register.

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "You cannot ask to remove your company’s registered office address, even if it’s your home address.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }} {{ govukInsetText({ text: "It costs £32 to remove your home address from each document. For now, you can only remove your home address from one document at a time using this online service." }) }}

What you'll need

If you already know which PDF document shows your home address, you'll need to tell us the document details. If you do not know the details or you're not sure which document your home address appears on, you must check the Companies House register.

You'll need to make a note of the:

  • document filing type
  • document description
  • date the document was added to the register
Where can I find the document details?
View the PDF documents in the company filing history on the Companies House register to find out which documents contain your home address.

Document filing type

In the filing history, select 'Show filing type'. A new column will appear in the filing history called 'Type'.

Document description

This is in the 'Description' column in the filing history.

Date the document was added to the register

This is in the 'Date' column in the filing history.

You'll also need:

  • the applicant’s name, date of birth and any previous names used on the Companies House register in the last 20 years
  • a different address to replace the home address, unless the company is closed or the applicant is no longer acting for the company
  • the company name and number
  • a credit or debit card

It takes around 10 minutes to complete this application.

Before you start

Check the list of documents you can apply to remove your home address from.

If you want to remove your home address from more than one document, you’ll need to start a new online application for each document or complete a paper application (SR01).
