Use this service to apply to remove your home address, or part of it, from the public Companies House register.

{{ govukWarningText({ text: "You cannot ask to remove your company’s registered office address, even if it’s your home address.", iconFallbackText: "Warning" }) }}

It costs £32 to remove your address from each document.

You can use this sevice if you are, or were:

  • a company officer
  • a person with significant control (PSC)
  • a subscriber or member of a company, LLP, societas Europaea (SE) or eligible Scottish partnership
  • an officer or permanent representative of an overseas company

You'll need:

  • the company name and number
  • the address you want to remove
  • a credit or debit card
  • a service address to replace your home address (unless the company is closed or you are no longer acting for the company)
  • to know which documents contain your home address, including the document type and registration date

You can check the document details by finding your company on the Companies House register.
